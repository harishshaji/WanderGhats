Package Overview

The Nature Line package presents a captivating 10-day exploration of Kerala's most iconic destinations. Begin with thrilling treks in Wayanad, then delve into the vibrant street life and beaches of Kozhikode. Experience the traditional boat-making in Beypore and immerse yourself in local martial arts and crafts in Ponnani. Enjoy the natural beauty of Athirapalli Waterfalls, Munnar’s spice gardens, and the serene waters of Munroe Island. Conclude with a tranquil houseboat cruise through Alappuzha and Kumarakom, and a heritage walk through the historic streets of Fort Kochi. This package is meticulously designed to offer a harmonious blend of Kerala’s natural wonders and cultural heritage.

Daily Itinerary and Activities

Immerse yourself in Kerala's natural and cultural wonders with our Nature Line package. Start with an invigorating trek to Chembra Peak in Wayanad, followed by engaging in local crafts and exploring Jain temples. Delight in Kozhikode’s street food and beaches, then discover traditional boat-making and folk dances in Beypore. Experience martial arts and coir making in Ponnani before marveling at Athirapalli Waterfalls in Thrissur. Savor the flavors of Munnar’s spice gardens and explore Munroe Island with kayaking. Enjoy a peaceful houseboat cruise through Alappuzha and Kumarakom, and end your journey with a heritage walk through Fort Kochi’s historic sites. Each day is thoughtfully designed to blend adventure, cultural insights, and relaxation.
