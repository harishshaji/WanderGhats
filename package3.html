Package Overview

The Retreat Line package offers a refreshing 7-day journey through Kerala, perfect for those seeking a shorter yet enriching escape. Begin your adventure with a trek to Chembra Peak and explore the charming Wayanad region. Engage in cultural experiences such as bamboo craft making and pottery in Cheruvayal and Nellarachal. Savor Kozhikode’s street food and relax on its beautiful beaches. Discover the spice plantations of Munnar and experience the unique lifestyle of Munroe Island with kayaking. Unwind on a serene houseboat cruise through the backwaters of Alappuzha and Kumarakom. Conclude your trip with a heritage walk through the historic streets of Fort Kochi. This package blends Kerala’s natural beauty and cultural richness into a memorable and compact experience.
